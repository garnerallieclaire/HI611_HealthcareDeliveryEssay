# HI611 Healthcare Delivery Essay – Specialization and Health IT

## Overview
This project is an analytical essay exploring the impact of increasing specialization in healthcare and the role of health information technology (HIT) in addressing associated challenges. The essay examines how specialization affects care delivery, continuity of care, and the development of healthcare IT systems.

## Objectives
- Analyze the reasons for increasing specialization in healthcare
- Evaluate challenges specialization creates for health IT systems
- Examine the impact on quality and continuity of care
- Assess how health IT improves coordination and patient outcomes

## Tools Used
- Academic research and literature review
- Healthcare systems analysis
- Microsoft Word (documentation)

## Project Summary

### Healthcare Specialization
- Describes historical and modern drivers of specialization, including:
  - Advances in medical knowledge and technology  
  - Growth of chronic disease management  
  - Expansion of medical education and training pathways 
### Impact on Health IT
- Identifies challenges for HIT systems:
  - Specialty-specific workflows and documentation requirements  
  - Need for customized EHR templates and clinical decision support tools  
  - Increased system complexity across specialties   

### Interoperability and Care Coordination
- Discusses barriers to data exchange between providers and systems  
- Highlights the role of:
  - Interoperability standards and regulations (e.g., 21st Century Cures Act)  
  - Patient portals and data-sharing tools  
  - Population health management systems 

### Quality and Continuity of Care
- Examines trade-offs between specialization and holistic care  
- Explains how health IT supports:
  - Improved communication between providers  
  - Reduced duplication of services  
  - Better longitudinal patient care  

## Key Skills Demonstrated

- Critical thinking and synthesis of academic literature
